Interview Mark

Dennis asked us (Robin & Tessa) for a meeting with Mark Klerkx, so we could update him of the state of the project. Mark is the head of the Lectorate Interaction Design, and thus runs the project in name of the lectorate.

Conclusion

Mark was pretty impressed by what we've done and encouraged us to continue in the way we had been going. He said to look into different forms of nurses and to make personas from them. He also thinks that it's a good idea to present the project to the consortium to show the other interested parties from the project what we've done.
