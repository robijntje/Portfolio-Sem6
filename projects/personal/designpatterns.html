Design Pattern Research

For the development of my portfolio and to answer the research question ‘How do I make my portfolio easy to navigate?’, I wanted to see what kind of standardized practices already exist for websites. As such, I researched design patterns and noted down all of the patterns that are or can be relevant for my portfolio.

Full document

Conclusion

I found a lot of relevant design patterns that I can use for my website to make the navigation more comprehensive. I do hope that I still have enough time to implement them though, as the group project is taking up a lot of time. If time gets sparse, I will make a selection of the most important patterns to implement, while also looking at how efficiently I can add the specific patterns. This way I can still implement as much as possible.
