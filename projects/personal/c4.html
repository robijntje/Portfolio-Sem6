C4 Model Personal Project

To help me with the organization of the structure of my portfolio, I created a C4 model that illustrates all the parts of my portfolio and how they connect to each other.

Full document

Conclusion & Reflection

After creating the C4 model it became much clearer to me what my website is going to look like, and I made sure that I won’t miss any of the pages during development. I also think it would help with the communication between me and my stakeholders, as the C4 model is a widely used tool that most software developers understand. This way I can easily show the proposed structure of my application, and can stakeholders also easily give feedback on said architecture.
